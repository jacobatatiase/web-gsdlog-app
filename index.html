About GSDlog

GSDlog was born from a passion for German Shepherd Dogs and the desire to create the most comprehensive tracking and insights platform for GSD owners worldwide.

Our team of developers, veterinarians, and GSD enthusiasts have worked together to build an app that understands the unique needs and characteristics of this incredible breed.

From puppyhood to senior years, GSDlog helps you track every moment of your German Shepherd's journey, providing valuable insights that help you make informed decisions about their health, training, and wellbeing.

Support & FAQ

When will the app be available?

GSDlog is currently in development. We're aiming for a beta release in Fall 2025, with a full launch following shortly after.

Which platforms will GSDlog be available on?

GSDlog is built primarily for iOS (iPhone, iPad, and Apple Watch). An Android version may follow based on demand, and we're considering a web version for viewing your data and reports - though the core tracking experience will remain app-focused.

How does the health tracking work?

Our health tracking is powered by scientific research, official breed standards, and veterinary data specific to German Shepherds. Combined with specially trained AI that understands GSD-specific health patterns, we provide evidence-based insights tailored to your dog's unique needs.

Can I import existing data?

We're working on import tools for common formats and other pet apps to make transitioning to GSDlog seamless.
