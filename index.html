GSDlog — a privacy-first walk log for German Shepherds.

Built by a GSD owner, in active development with a small tester group.

In active development

What exists today

  • Reliable GPS walk tracking: distance, time, pace, splits.
  • Lock Screen Live Activity: Current walk + Today's totals, at a glance.
  • Sharecards for quick progress sharing.
  • GPX import and a safe Support Bundle export for troubleshooting.

Why GSD-specific

German Shepherds aren't generic "active dogs." They have rapid growth phases, joint considerations, strong drive, and thrive on multiple structured walks plus brain work. GSDlog is built around that reality: age-aware coaching, place & weather lenses, and breed-appropriate routines.

Coming next (insights & features)

Insights are opt-in, computed on-device where possible, and shaped with the community.

Trend lens

"Your distance fell 18% in the last 4 weeks while time held steady → average pace slowed. Any reason?"

Weather lens

"You walk about 10–15% longer on dry days than rainy days."

Place lens

"Industrial-park walks are 0.6 km shorter but 8 min longer — more sniff stops?"

Age-based advisory bands (opt-in)

Compare today's distance/time to age-appropriate ranges for a GSD. Customize with your vet's guidance. Gentle nudges when you're under your band; small celebrations when you pass it.

Consistency & milestones

Streaks, personal bests, and week-over-week progress — without nagging.

R&D: Growth check-ins (opt-in)

Weekly front + side photo to build a growth timeline. Starts with manual weight & Body Condition Score prompts. Future on-device AI may flag coarse trends like "weight trending up."

R&D: Stool check-ins (opt-in)

Simple consistency/color log (optional photo) to spot patterns after food changes. Trends and reminders only — not medical advice.
